Course: Reinforcement Learning

Semester: 7

Credits: 4

Description:
This course introduces intelligent agents that learn optimal behaviour by interacting with an environment. Topics include Markov Decision Processes, Dynamic Programming, Monte Carlo methods, Temporal Difference Learning, Q-Learning, SARSA and Deep Reinforcement Learning.

Applications:
Autonomous Robots
Game Playing
Industrial Automation
Self-driving Cars

Course: AI for Space Agriculture

Semester: 8

Credits: 3

Description:
This elective introduces autonomous AI systems for extraterrestrial agriculture. Topics include lunar greenhouse automation, Martian soil analysis using AI, autonomous crop monitoring robots, and reinforcement learning for controlled-environment farming.

Applications:
Space Robotics
Autonomous Agriculture
Planetary Exploration
